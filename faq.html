Is Slovakia safe for tourists ?

Slovakia is considered a safe travel destination. In fact, it's sometimes considered one of the safest countries to travel through in Europe. However, you still need to exercise basic safety precautions when visiting.

What is Slovakia famous for ?

Unique castles, caves, hot springs, folk architecture, the European Dead Sea and famous Celtic town, European wilderness and much more.

What is the national drink of Slovakia ?

Slovakia: Borovička is a juniper alcoholic spirit.

What is the climate in Slovakia ?

Slovakia experiences a continental climate with distinct seasons, featuring warm, dry summers and cold, snowy winters. Temperatures can vary significantly between the mountainous north and the lowlands in the south. The warmest months are typically June, July, and August, while the coldest are January and February.
